Journey of the Coffee Bean

If you asked us where the coffee beans that went into your cappuccino came from, we'd probably be able to tell you. The journey that the coffee in that perfect cup of yours has been on is worth writing about.Our milk comes from cows at Amul dairy and our coffee is hand roasted in small batches by Best Mile Coffee Roasters in North India. We are sure you know that the best coffee comes from freshly ground beans. That is why at a Cafe Aurora you'll hear the grinder go 'whirrr' for just 15-20 seconds for each shot of espresso. Finally, the last mile: the transformation of finely ground coffee into that perfect cuppa. There's a choice at that too.All roads that lead to that final ultimate objective: a great cup of coffee. From bean, to cup. Every time.
